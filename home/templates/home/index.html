  • Our Story

What is it about?

The power of machine learning...

Say you are tired of listening to the same songs over and over again. You want something fresh, something new, something you've never heard before. That's where our app comes in. Based on powerfull machine learning algorithms we can recommend songs you'll like but probably didn't come across yet.

Implemented models

Maybe you want to know more about the magic behind the scenes. We get it. Our models are not supposed to be blackboxes, if you want to understand them, you can.

The first model is a random model. Sure, it doesn't do anything good, but you know - baby steps. Random model simply samples 10 IDs randomly from our database. That simple.

KNN model is much more interesting. You may have heard about neirest neighbours algorithm which is part of this model. We have a dataset with user-song interactions. Thank to this algorithm we can take your chosen songs and find a user most similar to you. Then the model can recommend new songs based on this similarity.

And last but not least... Word2Vec model. This model is most used in NLP (natural language processing) and has the power to find similar words in sentences. If we take playlists as sentences and songs as words, we can use Word2Vec for recommendation.

Now you know everything you need to explore your custom recommendations. Let's go!
