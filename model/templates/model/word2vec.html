Word2Vec Model

This technique was developed by a team of Google researchers led by Tomas Mikolov. The patented algorithm was initially used in natural language processing to learn word associations.

Neural networks in this model learn associations between words from sentences. However the same approach can be used in music recommendation - just imagine playlists as sentences and songs in those playlists as words.
